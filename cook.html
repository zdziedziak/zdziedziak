Mount Cook Photos

Photos from the Mount Cook area

Mount Cook is New Zealand's tallest mountain. We also took a boat tour in front of the Tasman Glacier (NZ's largest glacier). We rode around a small lake, which did not exist before roughly 1990. If you look at the photos, there is a clear vegetation line - the glacier extended to that height around 45 years ago...
