Colmar

Photos from Colmar

Colmar is a beautiful town in Alsace.

Colmar is renowned for its well-preserved old town. It is located on the Alsatian Wine Route, and is considered the "capital of Alsatian wine" (capitale des vins d'Alsace). Colmar mostly avoided the destructions of the French Revolution, the Franco-Prussian War, WWI and WWII, and thus is a rarely intact city in the greater area.
