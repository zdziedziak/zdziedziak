Château du Haut-Kœnigsbourg

Photos from Château du Haut-Kœnigsbourg

Château du Haut-Kœnigsbourg (Hohkönigsburg) is a medieval Alsatian castle.

The castle is located in the Vosges mountains, and was used from the Middle Ages until the Thirty Years' War when it was abandoned. At the behest of Kaiser Wilhlem II, it was heavily renovated from 1900 to 1908.
